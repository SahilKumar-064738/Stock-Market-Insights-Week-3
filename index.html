Growth of the Stock Market Over the Years

Consumer Discretionary Sector Growth
This sector includes companies that sell non-essential goods and services, such as retail and luxury brands.
Consumer Staples Sector Growth
This sector consists of companies that provide essential products like food, beverages, and household goods.
Energy Sector Growth
The energy sector is driven by oil, gas, and renewable energy sources, and has seen varied growth over the years.
Utilities Sector Growth
The utilities sector includes companies that provide water, electricity, and other essential services.
Materials Sector Growth
This sector includes companies involved in the extraction and production of raw materials such as metals, chemicals, and construction materials.
Industrials Sector Growth
The industrials sector consists of companies in manufacturing, transportation, and infrastructure industries.
Real Estate Sector Growth
This sector includes companies involved in real estate development, investment, and property management.
Communication Services Sector Growth
This sector includes companies providing telecommunication and media services, which have evolved significantly in recent years.
